# 📊 Sales Store Analysis | Data Analytics Project
🧾 Project Overview

This project analyzes retail store sales data to uncover actionable insights that help improve business performance, customer satisfaction, and operational efficiency. The dataset includes transaction-level details such as orders, customers, product categories, delivery timelines, and profit metrics.

The goal is to transform raw data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Problem Statement

The retail business lacks visibility into key performance areas, leading to missed revenue opportunities and inefficient operations. Specifically, the business is unable to clearly identify:

Top-selling products and categories
Customer preferences and buying patterns
High-profit vs low-profit items
Issues in delivery performance and order fulfilment

This results in poor decision-making, customer dissatisfaction, and reduced profitability.

💡 Objective

To analyse sales data and build intuitive reports that:
Identify top-performing products and categories
Understand customer segments and purchasing behaviour
Highlight profit drivers and loss-making areas
Detect operational inefficiencies in delivery and order processing
